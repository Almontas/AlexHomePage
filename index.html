About Me

I am a Dominican born New Yorker who takes great pleasure in learning and growing as an individual. I am always looking for the next challenge and believe in being bold and never remaining stagnant. I enjoy thinking and talking about digital marketing, corporate strategy, valuations, movies and TV shows.

I currently work at Verizon as a product marketing manager. I specifically work on the Go90 Video app, currently helping merchandising and programming efforts. I also advise small and medium size businesses who want to enter new markets or want to expand into digital marketing.

I have an MBA from NYU Stern School of Business in marketing and hold professional certificates in digital marketing and front end web development as well as having a bachelors degree in philosophy.

I am very interested in customer acquisition, growth, TV and video related projects.
